Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out is until 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Do you offer long-term stay discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for extended stays. Please contact our reservations team for customized long-stay packages and discounts.

Are your training programs accredited?

Yes, all our training programs are accredited by the relevant SETA bodies (AGRISETA and BANKSETA), ensuring quality education and recognized certification.

Do you provide transportation services?

We can arrange transportation services upon request. Please contact us in advance to make arrangements for airport transfers or local transportation.
