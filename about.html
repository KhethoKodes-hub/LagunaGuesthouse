Our Story

Founded in 2012 by visionary leader Ms. Lorato Kurite, Laguna Guesthouse began as a humble hospitality venture with a simple commitment to excellence. What started as a dream to provide quality accommodation has since grown into a comprehensive service provider that touches every aspect of professional and personal needs in the Kuruman community.

Today, we proudly serve the Kuruman community and surrounding mining industry with integrity, consistency, and unwavering dedication to quality. Our journey is a testament to what can be achieved when passion meets purpose.

From our beginnings as a guesthouse, we've expanded our services to include SETA-accredited training, construction services, catering, laundry, and conference facilities - always maintaining our core values of consistency and integrity.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our growth and success

2012

Foundation

Laguna Guesthouse was established by Ms. Lorato Kurite with a focus on providing quality accommodation services in Kuruman.

2014

Expansion

Added catering and laundry services to meet the growing needs of our guests and the local community.

2016

SETA Accreditation

Received accreditation from AGRISETA and BANKSETA to offer professional training programs.

2018

CIDB Construction

Continued Excellence
