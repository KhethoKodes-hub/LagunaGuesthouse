Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What's the best way to make a reservation?

For fastest service, please call us directly at 082 923 0340. You can also email us at info@villaguna.co.za or use the contact form above.

Do you offer transportation from the airport?

Yes, we can arrange airport transportation upon request. Please let us know your flight details when making your reservation, and we'll coordinate pickup arrangements.

How can I get information about training programs?

For information about our SETA-accredited training programs, please contact our training department at 053 712 2281 or email training@villaguna.co.za.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and electronic bank transfers. For corporate accounts, we offer payment terms upon credit approval.
